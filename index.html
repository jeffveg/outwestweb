The Experience

Three Ways to Have the Best Night Out

Whether you're bringing the family, planning a birthday, or just looking for something new to do in Goodyear — we've got you covered.

🏌️

Blacklight Mini Golf

Eighteen holes of glow-in-the-dark mini golf set against a stunning Sonoran Desert mural landscape. UV lights, neon cacti, and a western theme that's vivid without being over the top — a genuinely unique experience you won't find anywhere else in the West Valley.

🥽

VR Escape Adventures

Powered by VRCave technology, our escape room experiences transport your group into fully immersive virtual worlds. Work together, solve puzzles, and find the exit before time runs out. Great for families, corporate team-building, and date nights that are anything but ordinary.

⚔️

VR Arena Games

Step into the arena and compete head-to-head in multiplayer VR games designed for groups of all ages. Fast-paced, laugh-out-loud fun that brings out everyone's competitive side — and strengthens the bonds that matter most.

Our Mission

Strengthen relationships of family and friends by providing fun competitive activities

— Jeff & Alisa Schmidt, Founders
